### Bullet points

- Use when sequence is **NOT** important
- Can use either - or  * for the bullet points.

* Point 1
* Point 2
  *  Sub point 1
     *   sub-sub point 1

- Point 1
- Point 2


### Numbering 
1. First instruction
2. Second instruction
   1.  Sub step 1
   2.  Sub step 2
        * Point for sub step 2
